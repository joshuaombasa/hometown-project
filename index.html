Visit Enkare Nairobi

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Kenya.

Top three activities to do at Enkare Nairobi

Attend a show or a concert

The Sarakasi Theatre is a good place to spend your night out.

Go for a piknic in the local park.

The Uhuru park is perfect for piknic, geting some tan or just chilling out.

Get sweaty at the sports field.

Play volleyball, football, basket ball or lift some weights.
